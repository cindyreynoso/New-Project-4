The Goal

The aim of our project is to uncover patterns in mental health global trends.

What we will examine

We will examine relationships between disorders, how they impact lives, and bring value to decisions made in policy or preventative care plans.

What are some depressive symptoms across the USA?

This chart displays how common depression symptoms are, and how often they are. The chart has two major peaks at Low Energy and Psychomotor agitation. Whether experienced daily or often, these are the symptoms people suffer from the most. From this, we can decide that any depression treatment: whether medicine, change in routine, or therapy, should address these symptoms first. Also, these symptoms should be some of the first to look out for in someone suspected of going through depression
