The Goal

The aim of our project is to uncover patterns in mental health global trends.

What we will examine

We will examine relationships between disorders, how they impact lives, and bring value to decisions made in policy or preventative care plans.

What is the Problem?

External factors such as gender, age, and region impact the prevalence of mental health disorders.
