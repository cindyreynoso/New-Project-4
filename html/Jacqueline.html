Global Mental Health

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make healthy choices.1 Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.

What disorders are most prevalent?

This figure charts mental illness against countries who have reported it in their population. According to the chart: ADHD, Cannabis Use Disorders, and Major Depressive Disorder are the most commonly reported across the world. No other disorder reaches over 100 countries, and only two disorders surpass 50 countries.

What are some depressive symptoms across the USA?

This chart displays how common depression symptoms are, and how often they are. The chart has two major peaks at Low Energy and Psychomotor agitation. Whether experienced daily or often, these are the symptoms people suffer from the most. From this, we can decide that any depression treatment: whether medicine, change in routine, or therapy, should address these symptoms first.
